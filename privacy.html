Ewig Fintech Pvt. Ltd. ("Ewig Fintech", "we", "us", "our") operates the Mokssh platform accessible at mokssh.in and related mobile applications. We are incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, with our registered office at Pune, Maharashtra.

2. Data we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Identity: Name, mobile number, email address, date of birth
  • Financial: Insurance policy numbers, EPFO UAN, investment folio numbers, bank account details (masked)
  • Documents: Aadhaar-linked eSign data (not stored — processed at eSign service), will content, policy documents uploaded to vault
  • Family: Names, mobile numbers, and relationship of nominated family members

2.2 Information we collect automatically

  • Device identifiers, IP address, browser type
  • App usage patterns (for Smart Alerts inactivity detection)
  • Payment transaction records via Razorpay (we do not store card or bank account numbers)

2.3 Information from third parties

  • DigiLocker: Policy document metadata (with your explicit consent)
  • EPFO: PF balance and employer details (with your UAN-based consent)
  • SPARSH: Service verification for Defence Gold (for eligible personnel only)

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and improve the Mokssh platform
  • To operate Smart Alerts — detecting inactivity and notifying your nominated family members
  • To generate and store your Digital Will
  • To process subscription payments via Razorpay
  • To send transactional notifications (OTPs, alerts, reminders)
  • To comply with IRDAI, EPFO, RBI, and other regulatory requirements

4. Smart Alerts — special notice

The Smart Alerts feature involves sharing your financial summary with nominated family members upon detecting inactivity. You explicitly configure this sharing, choose the recipients, and can cancel any triggered alert within 12 hours. We treat this as a lawful sharing under your prior consent. You may disable Smart Alerts at any time from the app settings.

5. Data storage and security

All data is stored on servers located in India (AWS ap-south-1, Mumbai). Financial payload data is encrypted at rest using AES-256. Access cards issued via Smart Alerts are time-limited (72 hours) and single-use. We maintain an immutable audit log of all data access events.

We are not liable for data breaches caused by factors outside our reasonable control, including but not limited to state-sponsored attacks, zero-day vulnerabilities, or user negligence.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only:

  • With your nominated family members — via Smart Alerts, as configured by you
  • With service providers: Twilio (SMS/IVR), Razorpay (payments), AWS (infrastructure), Anthropic (AI Advisor responses) — all under data processing agreements
  • With regulators: IRDAI, EPFO, IT Department — when legally required
  • In a business transfer — as disclosed under a subsequent privacy notice with user consent

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@mokssh.in with subject "DPDP Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days.
