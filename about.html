Why I built Mokssh

"When my father passed, we spent four months in EPFO offices, three weeks on IRDAI helplines, and two years resolving a will dispute — all while grieving. Not one of those things needed to happen."

That experience — shared by millions of Indian families every year — is the reason Mokssh exists. Not as a startup pivot. Not as a market opportunity. As a problem I lived through and refused to accept as inevitable.

The technology to solve this has existed for years. DigiLocker, EPFO APIs, Aadhaar eSign, UPI — India's digital infrastructure is extraordinary. What was missing was a product that pulled it together into something a family could actually use.

Mokssh is that product. The Continuum — our mark — is the promise: your financial legacy flows unbroken to your family, automatically, when they need it most.
