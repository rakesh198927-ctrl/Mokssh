Unique to Mokssh

Smart Alerts — Zero competitors. Everywhere in India.

No Indian fintech offers this. Smart Alerts is an inactivity-triggered, automated financial data delivery system. If Mokssh doesn't hear from you, your family is automatically notified — with everything they need.

  • You set your threshold

    Choose 3–30 days of inactivity. You control exactly what your family receives and who receives it.

  • We check in — twice

    SMS nudge, then an IVR voice call. Press 1 to confirm you're okay and the counter resets instantly.

  • Family receives the Access Card

    A secure, scoped financial summary via WhatsApp and SMS — policies, PF UAN, will location, claim helplines. With a 12-hour cancellation window for you.
